Some fun activities!

The Buffalo River offers a diverse range of exciting activities for families and individuals to enjoy. Visitors can experience a variety of outdoor adventures including floating, hiking scenic trails, camping, stargazing, and exploring the rich natural history of the area. Whether you prefer sunbathing, floating, or simply taking in the breathtaking scenery, the Buffalo River has something to offer every visitor.

Found below are some images with activies that you can do at the Buffalo River, as well as a video clip from CBS Sunday Morning that showcases some of the stunning and serene locations along the river.

Floating and Paddling!

Having a floating adventure with your loved ones is a great outdoor activity! Before hitting the river, it's essential to check the weather and water conditions. Additionally, make sure to have someone experienced in paddling with you to guarantee a safe and enjoyable journey.

Camping out!

Spending a night under the stars with your loved ones is a great way to have fun outdoors! Don't forget to pack all the essential items you'll need for your overnight camping trip. And, to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience, make sure to bring along all the necessary materials.

Hiking!

Exploring the trails and mountain ranges of Buffalo River with friends and family can be an exhilarating outdoor activity. However, it's important to take caution while hiking and be aware of your surroundings at all times. Always keep a watchful eye on the path ahead and behind you to guarantee the safety of everyone in your group.

Stargazing

Stargazing at the Buffalo River can be an awe-inspiring experience. Find a comfortable spot with a clear view of the sky, and allow yourself and others to be amazed by the beauty of the stars.

History and Culture

Make sure to check out all the historical elements when visiting the river. From rock erosion, to old buildings there is plenty of rich history entwined with the beauty of the river.

Safety Equipment

It's extremely important to prioritize safety and caution when visiting the river. Remember to bring all the necessary equipment for your chosen adventure and take appropriate measures to ensure a safe experience for yourself and others.
